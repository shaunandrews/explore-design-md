## Overview

This legacy campaign direction was for a launch microsite, not the current app. It favors large type, dark purple backgrounds, rounded promo cards, energetic orange CTAs, and expressive marketing layout.

## Known Issues

- This document conflicts with the current product direction.
- The palette is not approved for product surfaces.
- The type scale is too large for dense dashboards and settings screens.
- It does not include semantic status colors.

## Do's and Don'ts

- Do make the UI feel dramatic and campaign-like.
- Do use large rounded cards.
- Don't use muted admin-style layouts.
- Don't use compact tables unless absolutely necessary.
